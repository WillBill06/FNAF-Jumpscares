# FNAF Jumpscares

Over **40** different jumpscares across the FNAF series!

## Features
- **Standard Scares** – random characters from FNAF **1 to 6**  
- **Nightmare Scares** – intense static jumpscares  
- **Phantom Scares** – jumpscare followed by vision loss  
- **Golden Freddy** – rare and terrifying! Lasts over **15** seconds  

## Setup (Required!)

Due to copyright, the GIF, PNG, and MP3 jumpscare files are **not bundled** with this mod and must be downloaded separately.

1. Download the assets from this Google Drive folder:  
   [FNAF Jumpscares - Asset Pack](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1H8rwsSD49FEYYShJbP0T28Ok9-Y3AnHF)
2. Download/Copy the **folder**:  
   - Go to Steam, then Geometry Dash
   - Click on the gear icon on the right, go to Manage, then Browse local files
   - Open the geode folder, then config, then paste the folder here, replacing the empty one if it exists
3. Launch the game — jumpscares are now ready to trigger!

## Customization
- Assign **custom keybinds** to each scare type  
- Adjust **random occurrence chances** and intervals  
- Toggle random events on/off  

## Audio & Visuals
- Sound effects from the original games  
- Fullscreen animated GIFs and PNGs  
- Unique flickering and opacity levels
